Brief Introduction

DeuxYeux is a project by four business students of the college-university PXL.

At DeuxYeux we provide our beautiful customers with clothes that are top-notch and trending on the market. We have an array of clothing styles that go from old-school to modern day style vintage clothing. Jackets, pants, shirts, skirts you name it and we have it! Shopping is 24/7 available on our Vinted store.

DeuxYeux vintage does not focus on a specific age category or gender, but on people with the same mindset. From enthusiasts for enthusiasts.
